Cole & Son’s Flagship Gallery
3 Jubilee Place, Chelsea, London

Since 1875 Cole & Son has been creating exceptional wallpapers with savoir faire. Throughout its almost 150-year history the design house has worked with best-in-class artisans and now – in 2023 – the brand has found a new home in the heart of Chelsea.

In 2022, the year of HRH Queen Elizabeth II’s Platinum Jubilee, the design house moved from north London into new premises on Jubilee Place SW3 which, for 30 years prior, housed an art gallery.

The building was originally built in 1888 for the purpose of wallpaper manufacture making Cole & Son’s move truly authentic; the house finally reverting to its original purpose. Now, the impressive Victorian building will be the first ever flagship for the design house, with a 200 square metre ground floor gallery with its extraordinary ceiling height displaying the brand’s vast portfolio of precious art pieces including wallpapers, fabrics, and ‘ready to style’ finished goods.

The house is also home to a new by-invitation-only archive library, a sumptuous room holding designs, wood blocks, and archival documents belonging to the brand from the past 4 centuries.
